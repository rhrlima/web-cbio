The COMP-MOEAS Project



The project "Competent multi-objective optimization methods based on evolutionary algorithms and statistical modeling techniques" is financed as part of the program Science Without Borders. A joint effort of the Brazilian Ministries of Education (MEC), and Science and Technology (MCT) through their respective funding agencies-CAPES and CNPq.

The main objective of this project is to develop methodological and theoretical results for the solution of general and complex multi-objective optimization problems (MOPs) with advanced optimization methods based on evolutionary algorithms and probabilistic graphical models.
