Objectives

The project comprises five main objectives:

  • 1) Design of competent multi-objective evolutionary algorithms that incorporate probabilistic modeling.
  • 2) Application of Comp-MOEAs to the analysis of human brain recordings with application to the study of neurological diseases and brain computer interfaces.
  • 3) Development of a theoretical model for the analysis of Comp-MOEAs based on the extension of the theory of quantitative genetics.
  • 4) Network analysis of probabilistic models learned by Comp-MOEAs with the goal of knowledge extraction.
  • 5) Implementation of software platforms for Comp-MOEAs in Matlab and Python to introduce their use in industrial, teaching, and research applications.
